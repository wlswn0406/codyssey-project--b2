- Biomni leverages advanced AI models to identify novel aging biomarkers, create personalized longevity strategies, and simulate biological experiments.
- This innovative AI aims to revolutionize longevity science by reducing research time and cost, potentially helping humanity extend healthy lifespan and overcome aging.
