- Suprema, a leader in biometrics and security, became the first in its Korean industry to acquire ISO/IEC 42001 certification for AI management systems.

- The certification, applied to Suprema's AI-based security systems, is expected to enhance customer trust and global competitiveness by reinforcing responsible AI.
